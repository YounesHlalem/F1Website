2017 FORMULA 1 AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX

Vrij - Zon: €1900

A large part of the track excitement will be performed right before your eyes at this state-of-the-art facility located by the challenging chicane leading out of turn one. The Chicane Pavilion features an impressive range of suites over two levels that will take the hospitality and entertainment of the Australian Grand Prix one step further for its guests. From the lower part of the Chicane Pavilion, you will enjoy close-up views of turns 1 and 2 as well as the main straight through the suite’s expansive windows, from the outdoor viewing deck or at your reserved grandstand seat.
